# Research Proposals

These are paper-ready future-work proposals that fit the current project.

## 1. Prospective Rural Validation Study

Evaluate the dashboard in a real rural screening workflow with community health workers, comparing screening outcomes, usability, and referral follow-up against the current questionnaire-only baseline.

## 2. Multilingual Generalization Study

Measure performance and calibration across English, Hindi, and Bengali, including the quality of translated questionnaire labels, recommendation text, and user comprehension.

## 3. Multimodal Ablation Study

Test the contribution of each modality separately:

- questionnaire only,
- questionnaire + text,
- questionnaire + text + audio,
- questionnaire + text + image,
- full multimodal system.

## 4. Offline-First Reliability Study

Measure how often the dashboard can complete a usable assessment when the backend is temporarily unavailable, and quantify sync latency, data loss risk, and recovery behavior.

## 5. Fairness and Bias Audit

Examine whether performance differs by language, region, gender, age group, or recording quality, and report calibration and error patterns across subgroups.

## 6. Clinical Utility Study

Assess whether the dashboard improves time-to-screen, documentation completeness, follow-up planning, and clinician confidence in resource-limited settings.
