# Paper Submission Checklist

- Title and abstract finalized.
- Author list and affiliations finalized.
- All claims in the manuscript have supporting citations.
- Table values and figure labels match the final evaluation outputs.
- Ethics and consent language reviewed.
- Data availability statement completed.
- Demo records and synthetic examples are clearly labeled or excluded.
- PDF export is visually checked.
- Bibliography is complete and verified.
- Spelling and grammar pass completed.
