Intake Flow

Complete one screening from start to finish

Enter the person’s details, capture their responses, review the live preview, and save one finalized assessment for analysis.

Step 1

Capture profile and consent

Start with identity, village, assessor, language, and consent so the screening record is complete.

Step 2

Collect symptoms and narrative

Use the questionnaire, written response, and optional speech or face inputs to build a stronger assessment.

Step 3

Provide supporting inputs

Add either a written response or a speech recording. For the image input, you can capture a live photo or upload an image file.

Step 4

Review and save for analysis

Once saved, the result moves into Analytics Hub and Records and Reports for visualization and follow-up.
